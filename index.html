CEX Route — Easiest

Best for beginners or high volume. Requires KYC.

INSTANT
1 Swap all assets into ATOM on Osmosis
osmosis.zone
2 From ATOM on Osmosis to ATOM on Cosmos Hub
3 Deposit ATOM to a CEX and swap to SOL
Binance, Bybit, Coinbase — or any other CEX that supports native ATOM deposits
4 Get Solflare wallet open_in_new
Install extension and create your wallet
5 Copy your Solflare address → withdraw SOL from CEX
Select Solana network when withdrawing
6 Stake SOL · Explore DeFi · Whatever — NFA

DEX Route — Trustless

Keep control of your keys. No KYC required.

PERMISSIONLESS

app.squidrouter.com
2 Get Solflare wallet extension open_in_new

3 Bridge USDC Cosmos → Solana via Noble Express open_in_new
