01 — What is Solana

Solana is the leading high-performance network powering internet capital markets, payments, and crypto applications.

One network. Thousands of applications. Sub-cent transactions. Here's what you need to know.

SOL
The native token

SOL is the fuel of the network. Every transaction, swap, or app interaction costs a tiny amount of SOL — usually less than $0.00025. You need SOL to do anything on-chain.

<$0.01
Transaction fees

Every action on Solana costs a tiny fee paid in SOL. The average is around $0.00025. Fast and cheap by design, not by luck.

Staking
Earn while you hold

Stake SOL directly inside Solflare — tap Stake, pick a validator, done. Earn rewards for helping secure the network. No third-party app needed.

Governance
Under development

In the future, SOL will be used to vote on key protocol upgrades and community decisions. Your stake will be your voice in how the network evolves.
